Personality dashboard

Intelligent personality insights automatically.


Increase your emotional self-awareness using this interactive dashboard that gives you a comprehensive thirty-factor personality analysis. This feature is completely automatic. Just sync your emails and recieve a full blown dashboard within minutes.

Benefits: No more hassle of taking personality tests. Reduce the chances of results being skewed due to tricking test systems, mood variations, and so on. Recieve more accurate insights.

Email Tone Analyzer

Dynamic semantic analyis of your emails.


Using top-notch natural language processing tools, you'll receive a visualization of the tonal analysis of every email you send or receive. This feature also allows you to see which words convey certain emotions or characteristics.

The intuitive hover-and-view interface enables user-friendly control and display. We also give you the flexibilty to choose your list of contacts for which this feature needs to be implemented.
